Honey Garlic Chicken (Easy Skillet Recipe)

This honey garlic chicken is sweet, savory, and perfectly caramelized on the outside while staying tender inside. Cooked in under 30 minutes, it’s ideal for weeknight dinners and pairs beautifully with rice, vegetables, or noodles.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless chicken thighs (or breasts)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • ¼ cup honey
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar or lemon juice
  • Optional: sesame seeds and green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken
    1. Season the chicken with salt and pepper.
    2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
    3. Cook the chicken for 5–7 minutes per side until golden and cooked through.
  2. Make the Honey Garlic Sauce
    1. Reduce the heat and add minced garlic; cook for 30 seconds.
    2. Stir in honey, soy sauce, and vinegar.
    3. Simmer for 2–3 minutes until thickened.
  3. Coat and Serve
    1. Spoon the sauce over the chicken until well coated.
    2. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onions.
    3. Serve with rice or steamed vegetables.
