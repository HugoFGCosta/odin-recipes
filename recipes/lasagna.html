Lasagna

This classic beef lasagna is rich, comforting, and packed with layers of tender pasta, savory meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. Perfect for family dinners or special occasions, it’s a dish that brings both warmth and flavor to the table.

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce

  • 1 lb (500 g) ground beef
  • 1 lb (500 g) ground beef
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz / 400 g) tomato sauce
  • 1 diced tomato (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil

For the Béchamel Sauce

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups (500 ml) milk
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • A pinch of nutmeg
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan (optional)

For Assembly

  • 10–12 lasagna noodles (pre-cooked or oven-ready)
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce
    1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet.
    2. Sauté the onion and garlic until soft.
    3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
    4. Stir in the tomato sauce and diced tomato.
    5. Add salt, pepper, oregano, and basil.
    6. Simmer for 10–15 minutes and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Béchamel Sauce
    1. In a saucepan, melt the butter.
    2. Add the flour and stir for 1 minute.
    3. Slowly pour in the milk, whisking constantly.
    4. Cook until thickened.
    5. Add salt, pepper, nutmeg, and Parmesan.
    6. Stir well and remove from heat.
  3. Assemble the Lasagna
    1. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish..
    2. Add a layer of lasagna noodles.
    3. Spread meat sauce, then béchamel, then mozzarella.
    4. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used.
    5. Finish with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  4. Bake the Lasagna
    1. Bake in a preheated oven at 350°F (180°C) for 30–40 minutes.
    2. For extra browning, broil for 3–5 minutes at the end.
