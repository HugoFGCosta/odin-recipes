Creamy Mushroom Risotto

A rich and velvety Italian rice dish made with sautéed mushrooms, Parmesan cheese, and a touch of white wine. This risotto is comforting, aromatic, and perfect as a main dish or an elegant side.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups Arborio rice
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups sliced mushrooms (cremini or button)
  • ½ cup white wine (optional but recommended)
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth, warmed
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Vegetables
    1. Heat butter and olive oil in a large pan.
    2. Add onions and garlic; cook until soft.
    3. Add mushrooms and cook until they release moisture and brown.
  2. Toast the Rice
    1. Add Arborio rice and stir for 1–2 minutes.
    2. Pour in the white wine and let it evaporate.
  3. Add the Broth
    1. Add warm broth one ladle at a time.
    2. Stir frequently, allowing the rice to absorb the liquid before adding more.
    3. Continue for about 18–20 minutes until creamy and al dente.
  4. Finish
    1. Stir in Parmesan, salt, and pepper.
    2. Garnish with parsley and serve warm.
